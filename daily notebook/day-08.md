## 💡 Big Insight

Certifications are tools, not the goal. The goal is skills and a working portfolio.
A strong GitHub with daily progress, real Bandit solutions, and working Python projects
carries more weight for a first junior role than any single paid certification.
Certification money should come from part-time work and savings over time,
never from selling family assets. Skills can be built for free starting today;
certifications simply confirm them later when affordable.
